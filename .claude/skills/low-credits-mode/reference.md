\# Reference — CLAUDE.Dev.md Parsing (Unstructured Notes)



\## Purpose



CLAUDE.Dev.md is NOT structured documentation.

It is a raw list of engine issues, limitations, and architectural problems.



Your job is NOT to understand everything.

Your job is to extract ONLY what is needed for the current task.



\---



\## 1. File Nature



Expect:

\- One-line issues

\- No categories

\- Mixed languages (French / English)

\- Multiple topics per line

\- File references at end of line



Example:

"Renderer2D — pas de rotation sur les quads"



Interpretation:

\- System: Renderer2D

\- Problem: missing rotation support
